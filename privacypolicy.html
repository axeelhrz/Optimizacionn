StarFlex Privacy Policy and Data Protection

1 Information We Collect

At StarFlex, we collect information necessary to provide our automation services for Amazon Flex drivers. This includes account information, usage data, and technical information about your device and application usage.

The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address)
  • Amazon Flex account credentials (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Device information and technical specifications
  • Application usage patterns and preferences
  • Location data for station optimization

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide, maintain, and improve our automation services. Our primary goal is to optimize your Amazon Flex experience while maintaining the highest security standards.

Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Automate block selection based on your preferences
  • Optimize schedules and station selections
  • Send notifications about available blocks
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

3 Data Security and Protection

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your personal information and Amazon Flex credentials. All sensitive data is encrypted using advanced encryption standards both in transit and at rest.

Our security measures include:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive data
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments
  • Strict access controls and employee training

4 Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide StarFlex services and is only shared in specific, limited circumstances outlined below.

We may share information only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or property
  • With your explicit consent
  • With trusted service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • In case of business transfer or merger (with prior notice)

5 Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your personal information and can exercise various rights regarding your data. We are committed to transparency and user control over personal information.

Your rights include:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted from our systems.

Our retention periods are based on:

  • The length of time you use our services
  • Legal and regulatory requirements
  • Business and operational needs
  • Security and fraud prevention requirements

7 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage patterns, and improve our services. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies for basic functionality
  • Performance cookies for analytics and optimization
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings
  • Security cookies for fraud prevention

8 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes through the application or via email.

When we update this policy:

  • We will post the updated version on our website
  • We will notify active users of material changes
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
